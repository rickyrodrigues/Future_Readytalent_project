Guidelines for Companies

1. Go to the "Post a Job" page.
2. Fill out all the required fields in the form. Make sure the information is accurate and up-to-date.
3.visit the form template page. copy the given form and makes changes as needed by you.
4. Link the form to a Google sheet. by following the given steps-
. Open the Google Form that you want to link to a Google Sheet.
. Click the Responses tab.
. Click the Google Sheets icon.
. In the Select response destination dialog box, do one of the following:
. Create a new spreadsheet: This will create a new Google Sheet and store the form responses in it.
. Select existing spreadsheet: This will allow you to select an existing Google Sheet to store the form responses in.
. Click the Create or Select button.
5. Share the link to the Google Form and corresponding sheet in the post a job page. This link will be used by companies to fill out the job form.
6. Fill out the job form and submit it. The job will be verified by the portal admin.
